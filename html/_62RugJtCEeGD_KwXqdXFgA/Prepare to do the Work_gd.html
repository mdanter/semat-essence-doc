Introduction

Set up the team and its working environment. Understand and commit to completing the work.

Prepare to do the work to:

  • Put the initial plans in place.
  • Establish the initial way of working.
  • Assemble and motivate the initial project team.
  • Secure funding and resources.

Completion Criteria

The completion criteria of this activity space includes the following alpha states to be achieved:

  • Team: Seeded
  • Way of Working: Principles Established
  • Way of Working: Foundation Established
  • Work: Initiated
  • Work: Prepared
